# Personas

## Purpose

This document defines the initial user personas for English OS V1.

The goal is not to create fictional marketing characters, but to anchor product decisions around real learner patterns.

## Shared V1 User Profile

English OS V1 is designed for self-learners who:

- study independently
- have access to many resources but lack structure
- want a system more than another content source
- care about progress and consistency
- are willing to return regularly if the product reduces confusion

## Primary Persona

### Beginner Self-Learner

This learner is early in the journey and needs confidence, structure, and a path that feels manageable.

#### Goals

- build a solid English foundation
- know what to study first
- avoid wasting time on random resources
- create a sustainable study routine

#### Pain Points

- feels overwhelmed by too many options
- does not know which resources are actually good
- cannot judge what is most important right now
- often starts and stops because the path feels unclear

#### Behaviors

- searches YouTube, apps, and websites frequently
- saves resources but rarely turns them into a consistent system
- often mixes grammar, vocabulary, and listening randomly
- benefits from clear structure and simple next steps

#### What This Persona Needs From English OS

- a roadmap that feels safe and understandable
- curated resources appropriate for an early learner
- dashboard guidance that answers "what should I do today?"
- progress visibility that creates momentum

## Secondary Persona

### Intermediate Self-Learner Who Feels Stuck

This learner is no longer a beginner, but their progress feels uneven and difficult to direct.

#### Goals

- break through stagnation
- improve active English, especially writing and speaking
- study more intentionally
- stop circling the same material without visible gains

#### Pain Points

- has consumed many resources but lacks one coherent system
- feels uncertain about weak areas
- struggles to turn passive exposure into active skill growth
- often feels "busy" in English without clear progress

#### Behaviors

- jumps between resources based on mood or algorithmic suggestions
- repeats familiar content instead of following a real plan
- can self-reflect but lacks a structure to act on that reflection
- is more likely to value analytics, review, and targeted practice

#### What This Persona Needs From English OS

- a roadmap with clearer prioritization
- resource recommendations that are more selective and strategic
- writing and speaking spaces tied to real progress
- review logic that surfaces forgotten or avoided areas

## Important Non-Target Personas For V1

These users may matter later, but they should not drive V1 decisions:

- advanced specialist learners
- test-prep-first users
- teacher-led classroom users
- children and parent-managed learners
- social-first learners looking for community products

## Persona Implications

These personas imply that V1 should emphasize:

- clarity over abundance
- curated paths over open-ended exploration
- strong beginner-to-intermediate usability
- practical guidance over academic depth
- writing and speaking as high-value active zones
