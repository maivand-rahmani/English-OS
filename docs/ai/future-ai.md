# Future AI

## Purpose

This document defines how AI may become a deeper part of English OS after V1.

Future AI should deepen the operating system, not replace it.

## Long-Term AI Vision

Long-term, AI should make English OS feel personally aware of the learner.

It should understand:

- habits
- pace
- retention
- weak areas
- avoided skills
- output quality
- review behavior
- resource effectiveness

AI should help the product adapt without making the product feel unpredictable.

## Post-V1 AI Directions

### Adaptive Roadmap Updates

AI can help propose roadmap adjustments based on:

- repeated difficulty
- fast completion
- avoidance patterns
- writing and speaking evidence
- review weakness

The system should still keep roadmap changes explainable.

### Retention Analysis

AI can help detect:

- forgotten material
- fragile skills
- repeated review failures
- blocks that need reinforcement

This should build on the review and learning event system.

### Deeper Writing Intelligence

Future writing AI can support:

- long-term mistake memory
- rewrite comparison
- personalized writing drills
- tone and naturalness coaching
- progress over multiple attempts

### Deeper Speaking Intelligence

Future speaking AI can support:

- richer transcript analysis
- repeated phrase pattern detection
- response structure coaching
- confidence trend summaries
- later audio-based pronunciation feedback

### Personal Planning

AI can help create:

- weekly plans
- catch-up plans
- review-heavy plans
- output-focused plans
- low-energy study plans

These should remain tied to roadmap and learner history.

## Requirements Before Deeper AI

Before AI becomes more central, English OS needs:

- reliable learning events
- clear progress model
- review history
- structured writing feedback
- structured speaking feedback
- stable resource and roadmap metadata

Without these, AI would be guessing instead of reasoning from product state.

## What To Avoid

Future AI should avoid:

- hallucinated resources
- unexplained roadmap changes
- over-personalized advice without evidence
- replacing curated resource quality
- hiding core product logic inside prompts

## Future Success Criteria

Future AI is successful if:

- the learner feels the product remembers them
- recommendations become more precise
- review becomes more timely
- writing and speaking practice become more targeted
- the system remains explainable and calm
